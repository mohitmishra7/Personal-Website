About Me

I am Mohit Mishra, a Civil Engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal. My work sits at the intersection of engineering precision and forward-thinking design — crafting transport systems that are not just functional, but resilient and human-centred.

My research focuses on sustainable mobility and road safety, areas where I believe thoughtful engineering can have a profound societal impact. I approach each project with both analytical rigour and a sensitivity to the communities these structures serve.

Beyond technical work, I'm deeply committed to the idea that infrastructure is fundamentally about people — how they move, connect, and thrive in the spaces we build.
